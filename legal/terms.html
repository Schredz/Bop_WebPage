2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

Bop is a daily music-sharing social media platform that operates on a unique "contribute-to-consume" model. Users must post one daily "Bop" (song) before accessing the social feed. This requirement is fundamental to the Service and cannot be circumvented.

The Service allows users to:

  • Share daily music posts with captions and up to three optional images
  • Discover and interact with other users' music posts
  • Access 30-second music previews from integrated platforms
  • Follow other users and build social connections through public or private profiles
  • Like, comment, and engage with content
  • Explore music through our discovery features
  • Build a personal library of liked posts

The Service integrates with third-party music platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, and potentially other services to provide song metadata, album art, and audio previews.
